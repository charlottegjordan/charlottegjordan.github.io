Embroidery Projects

I learned a couple embroidery stitches a while ago, but this past winter break was the first time since then that I practiced. Embroidery is really addictive, and I find kits a great way to practice different stitches/techniques.

Here's what I have so far!

"Full Heart" Kit from cozy blue handmade

I discovered this specific brand of embroidery kit from my seasonal job at an art gallery, where I get to make examples of the kits we sell. Now I'm a huge fan of cozy blue handmade!

Here's their website if you want to try them out!
