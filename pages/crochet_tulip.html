Tulip Tote Bag


Update 6/9/25


I chose to do this project literally immediately after finishing the daisy blanket, after I had spent a couple days scoping out some inspo pics like the one above on Pinterest.



I chose this pattern to do the project and adapted the warm pink gradient it depicts over the monochromatic theme of the inspo pic.


The rougher edges of the pattern made me want to even the sides out with a border that might make the process of sewing the panels together easier. I tried a couple different methods of cleaning up the edges, but everything I tried looked ugly to me, so for now I'm leaving them rougher.

I have both panels done, so all that is left to do is crochet the straps and sew everything together. I'm also hoping to sew a fabric lining on the inside with some light pink fabric leftover from my rag quilt. I didn't want to carry all the moving pieces abroad over the summer with me, so like the rag quilt, this project is on pause until I get home.

Project Dates: 2/13/25 - Present
