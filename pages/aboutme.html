Hi, I'm Charlotte!

Welcome to my portfolio/blog! Many of my hobbies have long involved arts and crafts, and with this website, I am hoping to document and be more thoughtful about my creation processes. In my blog, I'm looking to engage more intentionally with the art, artists, and art-centered discussions I've long appreciated but rarely taken time to stop and ponder meaningfully. In hand-coding this entire website, I'm hoping to get more comfortable with web design and self-led coding projects. All of this is me trying to grow as a crafter, writer, coder, and art-appreciator, and hopefully you enjoy my projects and rants! :)
